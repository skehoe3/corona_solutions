What is this place?

Find yourself with too much do? Or not enough? Many people or stores (grocery stores, for example) find themselves with way more to do than usual, and are overwhelmed. On the other hand, some people (waiters, for example) find their hours cut as business operating hours have been slashed due to the pandemic. We are here to help. Our site provides a way to match people who need help with those who have time to do so.

How to?

If you find yourself overwhelmed and need extra hands, fill out our I need help! form

If you find yourself with too much time on your hands and need something to do, fill out our I want to help! form
